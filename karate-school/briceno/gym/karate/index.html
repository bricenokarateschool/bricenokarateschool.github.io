Class Detail

Briceno Karate in Hialeah, Florida is a renowned institution dedicated to the art of karate. Led by instructors German Briceno and Melanye Altuve, our programs offer precise techniques, focused drills, and engaging workouts. Our classes include dynamic warm-ups, technique training, kata practice, sparring or partner drills, conditioning exercises, cool-downs, and meditation.
